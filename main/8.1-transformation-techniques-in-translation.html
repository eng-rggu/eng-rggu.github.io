8.1 Transformation Techniques in Translation

Содержание > Unit 4 > Chapter 8


   The transformations given below are caused by differences in the grammar and vocabulary of the source language and the target one.  At the sentence level, the translator deals with the following transformations:

1. omission,
2. addition or meaning extension,
3. transposition,
4. change of grammatical forms,
5. concretization,
6. generalization,
7. antonymic translation,
8. metonymic translation,
9. sentence integration,
10. sentence fragmentation.
 
Here are some examples of the recommendable transformations:
           1) Omission.
Sometimes it is possible to omit certain parts of the original sentence without translation.
Ex.: The key question to be asked today is whether we should use this method in all the experiments or not.
-Основной  вопрос заключается в том, надо ли  использовать этот метод во всех экспериментах.

           2) Addition or meaning extension.  
Sometimes the translator must add some information to make everything clear.            
Ex.: Perhaps the analyses have become too formal.
- Возможно, проведенные в этом направлении теоретические исследования носят слишком формальный характер.

          3)Transposition .
Due to transformation it is possible not to follow the word order of the original sentence and to replace words in the translation.
Ex.:  Manufacturers design the CPU to control and carry out basic instructions for their particular computer.
- С целью осуществления контроля и выполнения основных действий каждый производитель проектирует свое ЦПУ для конкретного компьютера.

           4) Change of grammatical forms.  
According to this type of transformation there should be done some changes of grammatical forms to make the translation sound perfect Russian. For example, the original Passive Voice construction may be changed into an Active Voice construction.
Ex.: A mechanical way of multiplying and dividing was devised by J. Napier, a Scotsman.
- Шотландец Напье изобрел механический способ умножения и деления.

           5)Concretization.
Concretization means that the original word with a broader meaning should be translated as the word with a narrower meaning.
Ex.: The problem looks easy.
-Задача кажется простой.

           6)Generalization.
According to this kind of  transformation the original word with narrower meaning should be translated as the word with broader meaning.
Ex.: Several exotic linear integrated circuits have been designed for specific applications.
- Несколько нетипичных интегральных схем предназначено для специальных применений.

          7)Antonymic translation.
This transformation means that an original phrase or clause containing a negation may be translated as an affirmative statement or vice versa.
Ex.: Such situation is not impossible.
- Такая ситуация вполне возможна.

Sometimes when translating an affirmative phrase or clause it is preferable to replace it by a negative one, without changing its meaning.
Ex.:  To handle all types of information is possible for modern computers.
Для современных компьютеров обработка любой информации не представляется сложным процессом.

          8)   Metonymic translation.
Metonymy is a figure of speech in which one word or phrase is substituted for another one closely associated with it. It is widely used in the process of translation when one word is replaced by another one on the grounds that there is some kind of association between them.
Ex.: University broke up for the winter recess.
-Занятия в университете прекратились. Начались зимние каникулы.


          9)  Sentence integration.
This type of transformation involves combining two or more sentences into one.
Ex.:  They did not present any information. Nor did they provide financial help.
-Они не только не предоставили никакой информации, но и не обеспечили финансовую помощь.

          10)  Sentence fragmentation.
This type of transformation involves splitting one complex sentence into two or more simple ones.
Ex.: We carried out a series of reactions with the raw materials being brought from their laboratory.
-Мы провели ряд химических опытов. При  этом исходные материалы были взяты из  их лаборатории.
